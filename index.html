# Lukas Wagner: About me

I'm a software engineer.
My main focus is on computer graphics, with specialization on low level WebGL and OpenGL rendering. I also have experience in human-computer interaction, mainly in developing high-performance haptics systems (both hardware and firmware). For both fields, I also have several semesters of teaching experience.
Currently, I'm a research assistant at the Computer Graphics Systems chair at Hasso Plattner Institute, Faculty for Digital Engineering, University of Potsdam, Germany.
